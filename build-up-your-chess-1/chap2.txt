=== Mating motifs

- **Legal's mate**: Opening, sacrifice a pinnend piece.

- **Damiano's mate**: control f7/h7 with a pawn on g6, then Qh7! (enemy king on g8 0-0).

- **Greco's mate**: c4-bishop, g5-knight and h5-queen.

- **Lolli's mate**: pawn f6, queen near, add rook/knight. Enemies pieces limit the escape root of their king.

- **Blackburne's mate**: knight g5 + bishop pair on diagonal. Enemy king on O-O

- **Pillsbury's mate**
