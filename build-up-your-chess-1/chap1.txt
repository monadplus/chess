==== Mating motifs

Mate along open lines (rook + queen or two rooks)
Anastasia's mate (queen + rook + knight)
The Arabian mate (rook + knight)
The queen-bishop battery
The queen-rook battery
Boden's mate (queen+bishop+bishop on queenside castled position)
